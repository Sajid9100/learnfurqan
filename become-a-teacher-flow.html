Do
  • • Introduce yourself by name & where you're from

  • • Speak slowly, clearly, and warmly
  • • Record in a quiet, well-lit space
  • • End with an invitation to book a trial
Don't
  • • Use background music or filters
  • • Include your phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • • Promote competing platforms
  • • Show low resolution or shaky footage
  • • Make it longer than 2 minutes
